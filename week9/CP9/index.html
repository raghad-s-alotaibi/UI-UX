About Virie Bakery

Virie is the Danish Bakery for all. Our bakeries are places where good food is celebrated from morning until evening, where there’s good taste in every detail, and where everybody is welcome. For us, there’s nothing better than taking a few honest ingredients and turning them into something special. It’s the best job in the world and we get to do it every day.
